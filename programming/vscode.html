Shortcuts to VSCode

These shortcuts are the ones I use regularly in VSCode on a keyboard with Danish layout. I hope some will help others, but I mostly made this page to help myself remember the basics between sessions.

Some shortcuts are not defalult (Shown in grey). These are added by going to File/Preferences/Keyboard Shorcuts and searching for the desired action (for example 'join lines').
Hover over the line and click the + sign. Choose the shortcut and confirm with Enter

ctrl+d Make an extra cursor after highlighted word

shift+alt+<arrow up/down> Add extra cursor (Use End to bring cursors to same point
and then jump words with ctrl+<arrow up/down> )

ctrl+shift+alt+<arrow down (Num keypad)> Copy line down

ctrl+shift+d Copy line down

ctrl+shift+q Split screen down

ctrl+j Join lines

ctrl+k ctrl+q Jump to last change

alt+<arrow up/down> Move highligted lines

ctrl+<arrow up/down> Scroll text

ctrl+shift+k Delete current line/highligted lines

ctrl+shift+7 Toggle commenting out current line/highligthed lines
